Acceptance

The service

ValidationCrew is a network that connects builders who create validation missions with humans who complete them and provide feedback. We provide the matching, verification, workflow and payment infrastructure — we don't author the feedback ourselves, and we're not a party to the underlying products being validated.

  • Verification may be required to unlock certain features, missions or payouts.
  • One person, one account — duplicate or fraudulent accounts may be removed.

Builder responsibilities

  • Describe missions honestly and lawfully, and don't request anything deceptive, unsafe or discriminatory.
  • Fund your wallet for the rewards you offer; approved submissions are paid from it.
  • Review submissions fairly and in good faith — rejections should reflect genuine quality issues, not a way to avoid paying.
  • Respect participant privacy and any confidentiality terms you set.

Human responsibilities

  • Give honest, original, good-faith feedback — no automated, copied or low-effort submissions.
  • Only claim expertise and attributes you genuinely hold; verification keeps the network trustworthy.
  • Respect the confidentiality of anything you're shown, including unreleased products and ideas.
  • Don't share, leak or reuse materials from a mission outside the platform.

Payments, rewards & fees

Builders pay the rewards they set plus a platform fee that covers matching, verification and payouts. Rewards are held in escrow and released for approved submissions; unused budget is refunded when a mission closes. Auto-flagged or rejected work isn't charged. Participant payouts are issued to the method on file, subject to applicable taxes and minimum thresholds. Fees and reward amounts are shown before you commit.
